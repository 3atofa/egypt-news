The arrival of "Al-Qahar" frigate

The Egyptian Armed Forces announced that it had received the second MEKO-A200 frigate, which was built by the German company TKMS in the German SBN arsenal, marking its entry into service with the Egyptian Navy.
Lieutenant General Ashraf Atwa, Commander of the Naval Forces, raised the Egyptian flag on the "Al-Qahar" frigate, which is the second out of four "MEKO-A200" frigates, which were contracted between Egypt and Germany, during a ceremony held in the German city of "Bremerhaven", in the presence of a number of commanders of the forces.
